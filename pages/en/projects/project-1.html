Why did i make this project?

I wanted to learn how game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine render 3D
graphics onto the screen of a computer

What did i learn?

I learned the essentials of rendering objects on the screen, requiring three key buffers: the VAO (Vertex Array Object), VBO (Vertex Buffer Object), and EBO (Element Buffer Object).
I gained an understanding of 3D object rendering, using 3D mathematics and the GLM library for OpenGL. Additionally, I focused on shader programming, exploring fragment and vertex shaders in the OpenGL graphics pipeline.
